How It Works

A streamlined four-step process from issue reporting to resolution

1

Report

Citizens report issues through web or mobile with location and photos

2

Review

System categorizes and routes complaints to relevant departments

3

Assign

Municipal authorities assign tasks to field teams with priority levels

4

Resolve

Teams resolve issues and citizens receive real-time status updates
